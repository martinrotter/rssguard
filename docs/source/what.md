What is RSS Guard?
==================
RSS Guard is an [open-source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_source), [cross-platform](#supported-os), [multi-protocol](#supported-readers) desktop feed reader and podcast [player](features/mediaplayer). It can fetch feeds in RSS/RDF/ATOM/JSON formats and connect to multiple web-based feed readers. It can also play most audio and video formats.

RSS Guard is meant to be simple and quite fast while also offering some features for power users.

RSS Guard is developed on top of the [Qt library](https://www.qt.io/).
